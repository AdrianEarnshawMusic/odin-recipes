Orange and Lime Margarita

This margarita, made with freshly squeezed orange juice, lime juice, and agave for a natural sweetener, is light, refreshing, and not too sweet. Salt or sugar rim optional.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces ofreshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ounce tequila
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • ice as needed
  • orange twist

Steps

  1. Pour orange juice, lime juice, tequila, and agave syrup into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice and shake well.
  3. Strain into a margarita glass and serve with an orange twist.
